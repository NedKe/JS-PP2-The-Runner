Welcome to The Runner game!

The runner starts running from the top left corner of the board. The runner on the board game moves based on the number shown on the dice. Start the game by clicking on the start button, then toss by clicking on the dice icon. In the block with a question mark, you choose an answer to the question. If your answer is correct, you receive two squares as a reward. You are punished if the answer is incorrect, and the runner has to go two blocks back. When the runner arrives in green squares with positive numbers is rewarded and moves forward. If the block is red with a negative number, the runner is punished and moves backward. You win the game when the runner arrives at the right bottom corner before the game time is over! Now click on the start button to play the game!
